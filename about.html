About Gladys Cakes And Store

Gladys Cakes And Store is a passion-driven brand that brings together two things every home loves — delicious cakes and reliable kitchen items. Whether you’re celebrating a birthday or upgrading your cooking space, we’re here for you.

We started as a small cake business but grew as customers began asking for more — from kitchen utensils to storage containers and even baking equipment. Now, Gladys Cakes And Store serves cakes and quality household goods across Nigeria, 100% online.

Every product is selected or baked with care, and every customer matters. We believe your kitchen and celebrations deserve the best — and we’re proud to be part of them.
